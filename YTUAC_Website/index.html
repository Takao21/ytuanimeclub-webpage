ABOUT


Founded: (Christmas) Dec 25, 2017.



Anime club was started by five anime enthusiast students with the purpose of introducing the anime and manga culture to university students. Plus, they wanted to share the fun times together with people of the same interest.

MEMBERSHIP


If you are interested in becoming a member, please contact the current club members or the Facebook Page. You will be given a membership form to register. Please read and fill it out carefully and submit it to an available round table member.



  • We will take a fraction of your memory... Just Kidding!
  • You have to be an active YTU Student to join.
  • You may have to stick a photo of yourself in the registration form.
  • A small amount of club fund will be collected each academic month so that we can organize events together. (Currently: 2000 KS)
